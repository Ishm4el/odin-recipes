Pancakes!

Description

Pancakes are quite possibly the greatest and most delicious plate to include as part of your morning breakfast (there has to be eggs too!). In any case, this page will go into detail in how to make pancakes in roughly 20 minutes; 15 minutes of prep, and 5 minutes of cooking; all of which, is relatively easy!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt, or more to taste :)
  • 1 table spoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melt

Steps

  1. Inside of a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Then, pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until it is smooth.
  2. heat a lightly oiled pan, over medium-high heat. pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using approximately .25 of a cup each. Cook until brown on both sides and serve hot.
  3. Enjoy!
